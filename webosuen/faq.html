How do you play osu？

The default buttons are the left and right mouse buttons and the Z and X keys. The circles that appear on the game screen are called strike circles. When the ring outside the circle shrinks into the circle, click it. Some circles are long sliders, you need to hold down click on the circle and follow it to complete the slider. When a large blue circle appears on the screen, click and hold the then rapidly spin the cursor around the inside of the circle.

The cursor seems to be a bit delayed.

Try enabling the hardware cursor in the settings. The hardware cursor is turned off by default as some browsers or systems may not support it. You can also adjust your audio offset or enter fullscreen if latency is still an issue.

The game is freezing/slow

You can attempt to change the resolution by disabling "use system resolution" in the settings. Make sure you are using a modern browser, such as Firefox or Chromium。

Nothing is displayed on the webpage.

If there is nothing displayed, that could mean the API, Sayobot (Chinese), is offline. Try reloading or checking their status.

A download manager opened multiple windows

This site uses XHR, please set up an IDM.
